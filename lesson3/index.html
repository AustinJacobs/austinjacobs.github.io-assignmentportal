Site Name

The site will be called Real Weather.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Real Weather is to provide up to date, real time weather information for its users. Real Weather takes the worry out of what tomorrows weather will be. Users have 24/7 access to weather information that they can use to make plans for future activities without wondering if the weather will be appropriate.
