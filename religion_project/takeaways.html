What Have I Learned?

Throughout my semester taking REL 250, I was touched by the Spirit of God in many ways. Mainly, I began better to understand the need for sacrifice in our lives. You see, Christ, prophets, and others have sacrificed so much to get to where the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is today. The least that I can do is sacrifice some parts of my life to help build the kingdom of God. I have a strong testimony of the power of sacrifice. My time in this class has shown me how everlasting Christ's Gospel truly is, and it makes me want to be truly prepared to meet Him again one day. There is nothing that I would not do to secure my place in the celestial kingdom with Jesus Christ and my family. I testify that Christ has given us all that we need to be prepared to reach that level of glory. Every day we must observe ourselves and determine what must be sacrificed so that we are ready to meet God. This class has been a significant influence in my life, and I feel more prepared to play my role in Christ's plan. I know that His everlasting gospel is true to the very core. It contains everything necessary to find eternal happiness. Of this, I am sure. In Christ's name, amen.
